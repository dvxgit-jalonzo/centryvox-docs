Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

01

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Diavox Network Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") processes information in connection with the Centryvox mobile application (the "App"). The App is a private VoIP and hospitality management tool designed exclusively for authorized clients of Diavox Network Inc.

02

Scope of Service

The App is intended for professional use by private clients. While it is distributed via the Google Play Store for efficiency, full access requires a proprietary QR code provided only to legitimate Diavox Network Inc. clients.

03

Information Processing and Use

To provide its core VoIP and hospitality features, the App requires access to the following device capabilities:

🎙️
Microphone
Used to enable Voice over IP (VoIP) calling functionality.
📷
Camera
Used exclusively to scan the activation QR code for authentication.
📋
Phone / Contacts
Accessed only to allow users to dial contacts within the VoIP feature. Data is never stored or shared.
🔔
Notifications
Used for incoming calls and real-time alerts (Fire Alarm, Wakeup Call, Room Status).

The App may also process limited technical information for operation, including:

  • Authentication data (e.g., QR-based access credentials)
  • Call metadata (e.g., timestamps, duration)
  • Device and system information for diagnostics and performance monitoring
04

Data Processing and Storage

Local Processing
QR code scanning and temporary session handling are processed locally on the device.
Real-Time Processing
Voice communication and signaling data are processed in real time to enable VoIP functionality.
No Persistent Storage
The App does not store personal user data, call recordings, or contact information on external servers.
Secure Transmission
Communication data is transmitted over secure channels to private infrastructure managed by or for Diavox Network Inc. clients strictly for service delivery.

We do not sell, rent, or trade any user data to third parties.

05

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect communication data handled by the App. As this App is intended for enterprise use, users are also expected to follow their organization's internal security policies.

06

Third-Party Services

The App does not include third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. It may use Google Play Services for essential functionality such as push notifications, crash reporting, and basic analytics. These services may collect anonymized technical data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

07

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on the appropriate distribution platform.

08

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly process data from children.
